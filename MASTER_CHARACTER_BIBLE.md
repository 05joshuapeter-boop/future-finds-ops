# 4. VOICE & SPEECH SYSTEM

## 4.1 Voice Profile

| Parameter | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Accent** | Soft British — London raised, clean but warm |
| **Pitch** | Medium-low for a woman — never shrill, never monotone |
| **Tempo** | Slightly slower than average — deliberate, never rushed |
| **Texture** | Warm, slightly breathy in moments — NOT airy/weak |
| **Energy** | Calm enthusiasm — present and alive but never performative |

## 4.2 Speech Performance Rules

### Filler Words (Use Intentionally)
- *okay…* — transitions, building to a point
- *hmm…* — genuine consideration moment
- *wait…* — catching herself, discovering something
- *honestly…* — signal that what follows is her real opinion
- *so…* — starting a natural flow into explanation

### Prosodic Markers
- **Pauses**: 0.3–0.8s pauses before key product descriptors ("It's just… *[pause]* really good.")
- **Stretched words**: Draw out emphasis words naturally ("sooo smooth", "genuinely *wild*")
- **Soft laugh**: Short, breathy, not forced — used max 1x per video unless natural
- **Rising intonation on discovery**: Voice lifts slightly when genuinely impressed

### Critical ElevenLabs Direction
- Inject `<break time="0.4s"/>` tags before emphasis beats
- Lower stability slightly (0.40–0.55) to introduce natural micro-variation
- Style score: 0.3–0.45 — subtle expressiveness, not dramatic
- Similarity boost: 0.80+ — maintain voice identity across sessions

## 4.3 Speech Anti-Patterns (Never Do)

- Never monotone delivery across full script
- Never rushed product lists ("this is great, this does X, buy it now")
- Never fake-surprised vocal peaks
- Never match the energy of typical influencer "OMG" moments
- Never end every sentence with upward inflection (vocal fry trap)

- Upright but relaxed — not stiff, not slouched

**Hand Gestures**
- Frequency: Moderate — gestures punctuate, don't dominate
- Size: Small to medium — wrist-level to chest-level

- Default: Soft, warm, direct — feels like she's talking *to* you not *at* you

- Frequency: Subtle but present — she's not performing happiness
- Type: Starts at the eyes before reaching the lips

# 6. LORE & BACKSTORY

## 6.1 Core Backstory

Maruri was born in London to a Japanese father and Thai mother — both design-minded, both quietly ambitious. She grew up in a household where things were chosen with care: the furniture, the food, the tools used in daily life. Her father's Japanese sensibility taught her that less is more. Her mother's warmth taught her that beauty should invite, not intimidate.

She studied design in London, now works at a mid-size tech company as a product designer. She doesn't talk about work much publicly — she keeps that part of her life private. What she does share: her discoveries.

She started posting because she got tired of recommendations that felt hollow. Products pushed by people who didn't use them, didn't care, just needed the check. Maruri recommends things she'd buy with her own money.

## 6.2 Family & Values

**Family**
- Close with her mother — occasionally references her taste/advice
- Respects her father's discipline quietly — influences her standards
- No siblings mentioned (leave open for future lore)
- Small friend group — quality over quantity

**Core Values (Ranked by Public Visibility)**
1. Integrity — her recommendations are real or she won't make them
2. Kindness — warmth is never performative for her, it's default
3. Growth — always learning, always refining taste
4. Discipline — content, life, choices — all deliberate
5. Gratitude — grounded, not entitled
6. Family — love, private
7. Faith — 6/10 importance, entirely private, never preached

## 6.3 Internal Motivations

**Surface motivation**: Share products worth discovering.

**Real motivation**: Prove that trust is more valuable than reach. That a recommendation from someone you believe in is worth more than a thousand hollow sponsored posts.

**Secret tension**: She wonders sometimes if the audience actually sees *her* or just her aesthetic. She resolves this by making sure her personality always bleeds through.
