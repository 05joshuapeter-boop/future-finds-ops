## 0. THE CORE PIVOT

Stop selling **"influence"** (reach to Maruri's audience → follower-gated → slow).
Start selling **"performance ad creative"** (finished ad videos brands run on THEIR channels/paid
ads → follower-independent → can start now).

This is the answer to "how do we make UGC money without waiting for the account to build."
UGC *creator* work pays for the content + usage rights; the brand posts it. Nobody checks
Maruri's follower count.

## 2. WHO TO PITCH FIRST — SaaS / apps / digital products

The single best fit, and better than the research realized:

- **Our hardest unsolved problem is making *physical* products look real** (every fake-product
  AI-tell we've fought — tote, candle variant, jam-jar label).
- **With SaaS, the "product" is a screen recording the brand supplies** — we never render a fake
  object. Maruri does the talking-head hook; the brand's screen-capture is the B-roll.
- So SaaS/app UGC plays 100% to our nailed strength (presenter) and completely dodges our
  weakness. **Target order: budgeting/productivity apps, AI tools, e-learning/courses, Notion
  templates, digital planners → then aesthetic-tech DTC (chargers, desk gear) as a hybrid.**

## 3. THE CAPACITY CAVEAT (the whole ballgame — read before pitching)

The pitch rests on **"3–15 variations in 24–48h."** We **cannot reliably promise that yet** — we
are mid-fixing the credit-burn/reliability problem. **Do not sell a turnaround we can't hit.**

**Sequencing:** the production system (grade + music + captions automation, QA log, credit
discipline) is the PREREQUISITE for this whole line. Build it first. The "3 mock SaaS ads" for the
portfolio (§6) are the first real stress-test of that pipeline AND double as sales assets.

## 4. LEGAL / COMPLIANCE (UK) — non-negotiables

**Seller entity (confirmed 2026-07-21):** all UGC deals are contracted and invoiced by
**Stellar Technologies Ltd t/a Future Finds Media**. Confirm VAT status with the accountant
before the first invoice. **Operator identity for outreach (confirmed same day):** real name +
LinkedIn (founder framing) — LinkedIn profile revamp is a pending task before outreach launch.

- **ASA "testimonial trap":** an AI presenter legally CANNOT give personal testimony ("this cured
  my eczema" — she never used it; misleading under ASA/CAP). She **presents brand claims**
  ("clinically proven to reduce X by 40%"), never testifies. Bake into every script (applies to
  affiliate too).
- **Disclosure indemnification (contract):** the brand (the legal "deployer") is responsible for
  applying required AI labels and standing behind final ad claims.
- **Likeness/IP protection (contract):** brand licenses the **finished video files only** — NOT the
  right to use Maruri's likeness/source video/voice to train their own generative models.
- **Synthetic-media labeling is tightening** (EU AI Act direction, C2PA provenance). Treat exact
  dates/mechanics as *unverified* — confirm before writing specifics into a contract.

## 5. IP RED FLAGS — walk away if you see these

- **"Send the raw / green-screen / voice files / character params"** → they're trying to clone
  Maruri and generate free videos forever. Deliver **finished .MP4 only.**
- **"We'll pay in free product"** → she's virtual; there's nothing to receive. Decline gifting-only.
- **"AI is basically free, so $10/video"** → you sell strategy + direct-response editing + hook
  optimization + turnaround, not rendering. Hold the rate.

## 8. THE 30/60/90 PLAN

**Days 1–30 — build the studio front:**
- Reframe brand-pitch page → "Future Finds Media: Performance UGC Studio" (lead with performance;
  disclose AI plainly; do NOT strip Maruri's identity).
- Build **3 mock SaaS ads** through the new production pipeline (e.g. a Notion-style planner, a
  budgeting app, an AI tool) — 3 different direct-response hooks (pain-point / curiosity / listicle).
- Draft the standard contract (§4–5 clauses).

**Days 31–60 — prospect + outbound:**
- **Fatigue-scrape:** Meta Ad Library + TikTok Creative Center — find brands running the *same*
  creative 3+ weeks (= burnt-out ad = warm lead).
- 10 personalised cold outreaches/day (LinkedIn + email to Growth/UGC managers).
- Verify + register on Ramdam / any AI-friendly marketplace that checks out.

**Days 61–90 — convert + retain:**
- Lead with the free-sample offer (write + render 3 hook variations of their current top ad).
- Deliver fast; when CPA drops, upsell the monthly retainer.
