# PERFORMANCE LOG
## v1.0 · 2026-07-16 · Phase 1.5 of BUILD_PLAN_GAP_CLOSURE.md

## Purpose
Weekly readout of real numbers, so decisions about what to make more of are based on what
actually moved, not vibes. This is the routine the user approved as a standing weekly Claude
task — read via Chrome (user logged in, read-only), logged here, and closed with ONE steering
decision: double down on X, drop Y.

**Rule:** every entry needs at least one decision at the bottom, even if the decision is
"insufficient data, no change yet." A log with no decisions attached is just a diary.
